About Butwal Multiple Campus

Established in 1972, Butwal Multiple Campus is a prestigious educational institution located in Butwal, Rupandehi, Nepal. As a constituent campus of Tribhuvan University, we are committed to providing quality higher education across various disciplines. Our campus has been a beacon of academic excellence, offering undergraduate and graduate programs that prepare students for successful careers and meaningful contributions to society.
